There were many data visualizations about Covid-19 in 2020. But graphs and tables just can’t communicate the severity of the problem or approach it from a humane position.

I made a 3D simulation of Kyiv’s city center where you can change the number of people, walk among them and see the crowded streets from the bird’s-eye view.

Women are blue, men are yellow.

Use joystick on the bottom of the screen or keyboard to move.

Click to change number of people and to open home screen.

If you get lost follow the lights in the sky. They will take you to the Christmas tree on Sofia Square.

Do you want to know how many people missed New Year holidays in 2021?

You can change the number of people, the position of the camera and return back to the city.
